About Us

About Our Company

About Us - At Terrenogen, we invest personally on every property project that we recommend to our investors. This is because we believe that we ourselves must sincerely believe in a particular project before others do.

Our own journey of investment as individuals started more than two decades ago with different challenges and success. We know investing alone will not be as profitable as those who invests as a group while management of properties as an individual will only lead to more headaches than profits.

Therefore, to overcome the challenges and to maximize rental yield, we:

1. Spot for good projects by doing due diligence and market survey

2. Invest as individuals but purchase projects as a group

3. Maximizing Rental Space through Creative Space Planning

4. Property Management is done as a Group where we have more control

5. Letting the projects out in bulk

6. Expanding the portfolios of our individual investors by ensuring stable rental returns and eventually aim to exit by going into a Real Estate Investment Trust

Our Duty

Our Duty

One of the areas that we are exceptionally good at is our ability to profile our investors according to their income and lifestyle before creating an investment goal and property portfolio for them. This is because we believe everyone is unique especially when it comes to investment.

We will then continue to monitor and manage our clients' portfolio and when the right property projects appears, we will prepare them to procure the asset with ease after our detailed briefing.

Our service continues by also ensuring that the property is renovated and transform to an asset where we can maximize the rental returns.

We continue to manage the property and their tenants so that it will be profitable and thus enabling the investor to procure future property projects without any doubts from their bankers should they need to.

We therefore aim for each of our investors to own more than a dozen properties before they reach their retirement age.

We are extremely effective in helping the following type of property investors:

"I am too Busy" - These type of investors are those who are either too busy working 9 to 5 or running their own business. They normally own a property or two and couldn't own more than that. Reason given by these type of investors is that they are too busy to manage the few properties and wouldn't want to invest anymore as they wouldn't have time to look and to manage

"I am losing $ in Property Investment"  - This is usually the single investor who loves and believes in property as an investment vehicle but due to a few typical reasons, lost money and probably still paying for it. Their reason may include:

a. Didn't get any discount on the property as it was a single purchase 

b. Didn't get a good discount on the renovation

c. Couldn't get tenants as their property agent was not focused 

d. Couldn't get good rental returns because the property was rented out with low rental returns 

e. Rental returns could not cover mortgage repayments 

"I am New" - They have not started their journey in property investment and they are unsure whether their 1st property should be for own stay or investment.

"I don't think I can afford" - This group of people are those who never really looked at what is happening in the property market and they are too quick to assume that they couldn't afford the deposit for a unit although they have a pretty steady income. They also assume property investment is only for the rich and those with extra cash.
